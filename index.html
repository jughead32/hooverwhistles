I'm Mac Hoover

My "signature sound" is quiet, but I make louder whistles, too. That typical Hoover sound is pure and sweet, but you can request it with just a hint of chiff. If you are looking for a whistle that you can play alone or with your friends without disturbing the neighbors, you've come to the right place.

"Whitecap", is my name for any one of my PVC or CPVC mouthpieces designed to be mated with a metal barrel of aluminum or brass of my making, or as a retrofit on any commercial whistle that has a lost, broken, or disappointing head.
